ABOUT US

Bakes & Cakes Hub is a world class confectionary and bakery of international standards.It is one of the few confectionaries in Uganda that was born to provide you with the best and unspeakably delicious pastries and snacks.It was started in June 2016 by Miss Mugeni Prossy who is the Managing Director/C.E.O. The confectionary started with only two employees and only one section/department. Currently it employees over 70 workers who work in the different sections of the confectionary.
